## E-commerce Sales Dashboard – $1.46M Full Analysis  
**Interactive Power BI dashboard** analyzing **$1.46 million** in sales from **5,654 orders** across a multi-category e-commerce business (2023 – 2025).

### Key Business Insights
- **Total Sales**: **$1,456,309** | **5,654 orders**
- **Average Order Value**: **$257.56**
- **Top 3 Categories**:
  1. **Sports** → **$319K** (21.9%)
  2. **Electronics** → **$295K** (20.2%)
  3. **Clothing** → **$289K** (19.8%)
- **Gender Behavior**:
  - Women dominate **Sports** ($189K vs $129K men)
  - Men lead in **Electronics** ($168K vs $126K women)
- **Top 3 Male Categories**: Electronics, Books, Clothing
- **Top 3 Female Categories**: Sports, Home & Garden, Clothing
- **Monthly Trend (2023-2025)**: Strong seasonality with peaks in March and June/July
- **Best performing month**: **March 2023** (~$78K)
